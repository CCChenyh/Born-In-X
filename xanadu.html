XIN has been living in Guizhou since she was a child, where multiple ethnic cultures mingle and reflect with each other, so that Guizhou has extremely rich and diverse ethnic cultural connotations and products. XIN has been influenced by her family's ethnic and classical arts since childhood. She is particularly fond of colorful ethnic embroidery, fine silver jewelry, ethnic costumes, ballads and other ethnic cultures, which have become the most important nutrients for her musical heritage.
XIN began to contact with popular dance and music at the age of 8, constantly absorbing and thinking about the lyrics, songs, production, choreography and visual presentation of the trend music. She pays attention to details and invests her body and mind. With extraordinary willpower and action, she starts from her spiritual core and integrates with the international popular music, hoping to create the trend dance music that belongs to China and integrates national elements.
Although they seem to be two completely different cultures, XIN is ingeniously combined and thoroughly integrated. She integrates her passionate feelings for national culture and music since childhood into the trend music, and connects the complete musical world view.
